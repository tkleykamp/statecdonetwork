About the Network

The State CDO Network is a project housed within Georgetwon University's Beeck Center for Social Impact + Innovation. We connect the top level data leaders in state governments to share best practices and support states in replicating effective data strategies. By convening and communicating across states, we seek to understand not only what's working, but how and why.

We are a non partisan collaborative focused on supporting state efforts to leverage data responsibly to support informed and effective policy making and to improve the delivery of critical state services.
